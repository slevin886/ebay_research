About Genius Bidding

Genius Bidding is a free to use market research tool to help optimize buying and selling on eBay. Genius Bidding allows you to search by keyword, category and more to gather marketplace data, key statistics, and interactive visualizations on the eBay marketplace of your choice.

Registered users can set up to three searches to run automatically, twice per week, and easily track trends in product pricing and availability. Genius Bidding also enables users to download full CSV data sets from their searches to easily conduct custom analysis on their own.

It's this simple to get started:

  1. Enter keywords that describe the item you want to buy/sell (e.g. 'Fender Guitar').
  2. Choose search criteria from a set of drop down menus to more narrowly define your target market.
    • Select by product category and subcategory (e.g. 'Musical Instruments').
    • Limit the results by listing type (e.g. 'Fixed Price').
    • Sort the results (e.g. 'Best Match').
    • Set minimum and maximum prices.
    • Limit the result by item condition (e.g. 'New')
    • Choose to return data from up to 100, 500, or 1000 items.
  3. When you're ready, click 'Get Item Data' to return your results!

What you'll get:

  • Instantly, you'll receive a page with:
    • Key Statistics: median price, minimum and maximum price, average price, the name of the top seller, total items available, % of items with a top rated listing, and more.
    • An ordered table of the Most Popular Items : the number of item watchers, images of the items, when the auctions end, and links to view the items on eBay.
    • Interactive Visualizations: a map of seller locations, a chart of the top sellers and key statistics on them, plots of price distribution, a chart of item characteristics, and more.
    • CSV Downloads: the ability to download a dataset with more than 50 variables describing each item.

For all of your searches, key statistics are saved and accessible via a table on your account page whenever you want them. For the marketplaces you're most interested in, you can set a search to recur automatically twice per week.

Over time, Genius Bidding will track your recurring search results and provide you a dashboard on your 'trends' page to show you how key marketplace characteristics are changing over time (e.g. how prices and the number of listed items are increasing/decreasing).

FAQs


What items are included in the results?

Genius Bidding gives you important information on currently listed items from sellers located in the United States.

Why do I need to register?

A couple of reasons: (1) Genius Bidding is most useful it if is used over time to track a particular product marketplace and you can only access that specific data over time if it can be attached to a unique account. (2) To ensure eBay's API limits are adhered to by setting a reasonable upper limit for each user's daily search count.

How do you make money?

Genius Bidding is not-for-profit.

Do you advertise?

No, there are no advertisements on this site and the only data Genius Bidding maintains are from your searches on this site and your account details.

Where is my confirmation email?

If you didn't receive your registration/other email within a minute or two check if it is in your spam folder.
